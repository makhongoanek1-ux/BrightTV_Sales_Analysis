# 📺 BrightTV Sales Analysis

---

## 📌 Introduction
BrightTV's CEO has set a key objective for this financial year: **grow the subscription base**.  
This analysis leverages BrightTV’s user profiles and viewership dataset (session-level consumption records) to uncover insights that will drive user growth and increase overall consumption.

The presentation covers:
- 📊 User and usage trends  
- 🎯 Factors influencing consumption  
- 🗓️ Content recommendations for low-consumption days  

---

## 📡 Total Revenue by Channels
- 🏏 **ICC Cricket World Cup (27%)** and ⚽ **Supersport Live Events (21%)** are the strongest revenue drivers.  
- 🎶 Channel O (12.66%) and M-Net (12.60%) also contribute significantly.  
- 🎾 Wimbledon (0%) and niche channels (<0.1%) generate negligible revenue.  

---

## 👥 Total Revenue by Age Group
- 👨 Adults (37%) and 👩‍🎤 Youth/Young Adults (31%) are BrightTV’s **core audience**.  
- 👶 Young viewers (7%) and 👴 Seniors (5%) show limited engagement.  
- 👩 Middle Age group contributes moderately (20%).  

---

## 🗺️ Total Revenue by Province
- 🌆 Gauteng (36%) and 🌊 Western Cape (20%) dominate revenue.  
- 🌿 KwaZulu-Natal (10%) and 🌄 Mpumalanga (9%) are moderate contributors.  
- 🏜️ Northern Cape (2%), North West (3%), and Free State (3%) show weak market penetration.  

---

## 📅 Most Active Day of the Week
- 🔥 **Saturday (16.55%)** and **Friday (16.42%)** are peak engagement days.  
- 📈 Wednesday (15.39%) also shows strong activity.  
- 📉 Monday (9.74%) is the weakest day.  

---

## ⏰ Peak Hours of the Day
- 🌞 **Prime Engagement Window:** 8 AM – 5 PM (peak between 10–17 hrs).  
- 🌅 Morning ramp-up: 4–7 hrs.  
- 🌙 Evening decline: after 18 hrs, minimal activity late at night.  

---

## 📝 Summary of Insights
1. 🏏 Sports events drive the highest revenue.  
2. 👥 Adults & Youth/Young Adults are the core audience.  
3. 🗺️ Gauteng & Western Cape dominate revenue.  
4. 📅 Friday & Saturday are peak days.  
5. ⏰ 10 AM – 5 PM is the prime viewing window.  

---

## 🚀 Recommendations
- 📡 Invest in **high-demand sports content** (e.g., cricket tournaments, local derbies).  
- 🎯 Target **Adults & Youth/Young Adults** with tailored content (drama, reality shows, music specials).  
- 🌍 Expand in **underperforming provinces** via localized promotions, partnerships, and community events.  
- 🗓️ Optimize scheduling:  
  - Premium content on **Fridays & Saturdays**.  
  - Improve **Monday programming** with new formats (talk shows, comedy blocks).  
- ⏰ Match content to **peak hours** (morning news 6–9 AM, live events 10 AM–5 PM).  
- 🌙 Reduce investment in **late-night slots** (post 11 PM).
